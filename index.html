Research Interest

Brain Computer Interfaces

Brain-computer interface is a technology that uses brain information to operate an external interface. This technology can provide new limbs to patients who are paralyzed and immobile.

Motor Learning

Humans quickly identify the target, select the appropriate motion according to their perception, and learn. I'm interested in this neural mechanism, experimental conditions for efficient motor learning, and motor aids.
